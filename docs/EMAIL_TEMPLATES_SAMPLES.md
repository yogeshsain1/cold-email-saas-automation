# Sample Email Templates for Job Applications

This directory contains example email templates you can use when applying for jobs through cold emailing.

## Template 1: Software Developer Position

**Subject:** Passionate {position} Seeking Opportunities at {companyName}

**Body:**
```
Dear {hrName},

I hope this email finds you well. My name is {candidateName}, and I am a {position} with {experience} years of experience in {skills}.

I have been following {companyName}'s work and am impressed by your recent achievements in [specific project/achievement]. I believe my background in {skills} aligns perfectly with your team's goals.

Key highlights of my experience:
• {achievement1}
• {achievement2}
• {achievement3}

I would love to discuss how I can contribute to {companyName}'s continued success. I have attached my resume for your review and would be grateful for the opportunity to speak with you about potential openings.

Thank you for considering my application. I look forward to hearing from you.

Best regards,
{candidateName}
{phone}
{linkedinUrl}
{portfolioUrl}
```

## Available Template Variables

When creating templates in the system, you can use these variables:

### Contact Information:
- `{candidateName}` - Your full name
- `{email}` - Your email address
- `{phone}` - Your phone number
- `{linkedinUrl}` - Your LinkedIn profile URL
- `{portfolioUrl}` - Your portfolio website
- `{githubUrl}` - Your GitHub profile

### Professional Details:
- `{position}` - Target job position
- `{currentRole}` - Your current job title
- `{currentTitle}` - Your current professional title
- `{experience}` - Years of experience
- `{skills}` - Your key skills
- `{degree}` - Your degree/education
- `{university}` - Your university
- `{industry}` - Your industry

### Company Details:
- `{companyName}` - Target company name
- `{hrName}` - HR/Hiring manager's name

### Achievements & Projects:
- `{achievement1}`, `{achievement2}`, `{achievement3}`
- `{project1}`, `{project2}`, `{project3}`
- `{skill1}`, `{skill2}`, `{skill3}`

### Custom Variables:
You can add any custom variables you need when uploading your contact list CSV file.

## Tips for Effective Cold Emails

1. **Personalize**: Always research the company and personalize your email
2. **Keep it concise**: HR managers are busy; keep emails under 200 words
3. **Clear subject line**: Make it easy to understand your intent
4. **Highlight value**: Focus on what you can offer, not just what you want
5. **Include attachments**: Always attach your resume
6. **Call to action**: Make it clear what you want (interview, call, meeting)
7. **Professional tone**: Be respectful and professional
8. **Proofread**: Check for typos and grammar errors
9. **Follow up**: Don't be afraid to send a follow-up email after 5-7 days
10. **Track results**: Use the analytics feature to see which templates perform best
